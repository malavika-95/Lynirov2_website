Email-first task completion — no client login

The feature that sets Lyniro apart. Tasks land in your client's inbox. One click completes them — no portal login, no app download, no friction. Completion rates go through the roof.

Built-in client chat — no Slack required

Messenger-style chat tied directly to the plan. Full history, online status, read receipts. Every conversation stays where the work is — not buried in a separate tool or email thread.

⚠️

Automatic at-risk client detection

Lyniro watches every plan continuously. Overdue tasks, stalled activity, multiple blockers — flagged automatically so your team can intervene before a difficult renewal conversation becomes a cancellation.
